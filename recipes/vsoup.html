vegetarian vegetable soup

Home

I make this super-easy vegetarian vegetable soup about every other week. It's thick and hearty, almost like a stew. Served with warm whole grain bread it makes a filling meal. Use green peas or green peas in place of okra if preferred.

Ingredients

  • 3 stalks celery
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 (15 ounce) can canned tomato sauce
  • 4 carrots peeled and cut into 1/4 inch rounds
  • 12 large potatoes cut iinto bite sized pieces
  • 1 cup frozen corn
  • 1 cup frozen shelled edamame
  • 1 cup frozen okra
  • 1.5 teaspoons salt, divided, or to taste
  • 2 leaves kale, chopped
  • salt to taste
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions

  1. Gather all your ingredients.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Cook and stir onion and celery in hot oil until onion is softened and translucent, about 5 minutes.
  3. Stir garlic into the onion mixture; cook and stir until fragrant, 2 to 3 minutes more.
  4. Pour vegetable broth and tomato sauce into pot. Simmer for about 10 minutes.
  5. Stir carrots and potatoes through the broth. Simmer until carrots are tender, 10 to 15 minutes more.
  6. Drop corn, edamame, okra, and kale into the soup. Continue to simmer until okra is tender, 5 to 10 minutes more. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. SERVE and ENJOY!
