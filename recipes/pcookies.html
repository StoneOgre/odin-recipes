3-ingredient Peanutbutter cookies

Home

Easiest cookies of your life! These cookies taste just like grandma used to make!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup sugar
  • (optional) 1 cap vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Gather all your ingredients.
  2. Mix egg and sugar
  3. Add in peanut butter and mix until thick dough forms
  4. Use spoon or hand to form golfball sized dougballs and place on pre-greased pan
  5. Use fork to press cookies and make the crossmarks
  6. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F
  7. Place in oven for 8-12 minutes
  8. Remove, allow to cool and enjoy!
